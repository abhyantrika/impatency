(57) Abstract : 
The present disclosure relates to a method performed in a communication network 1 comprising a Radio Access Network (RAN) 2 for 
facilitating resource management in the communication network of signalling relating to vehicles 4 which are connected to the 
network via the RAN. The method comprises detecting that a connected vehicle is stationary. The method also comprises obtaining 
location information about in which location the vehicle is stationary. The method also comprises correlating the location information 
with security information. The method also comprises determining number and type of sensors 5 in the vehicle which connect to the 
communication network when the vehicle is stationary in the location. The method also comprises allocating network resources to the 
vehicle based on the determining and on the correlated security information.
